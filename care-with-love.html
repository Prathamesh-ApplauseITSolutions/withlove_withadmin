Description

The "Sweetlings" program is a comprehensive rehabilitation initiative for children with Type 1 Diabetes.

It brings together a team of pediatricians, pediatric endocrinologists, dietitians, diabetes educators, psychologists, social workers, and peer educators, with volunteers playing a regular role in the clinic, Every Saturday, the clinic offers free services to underprivileged children at Jehangir Hospital, benefiting over 650 young-patients.
