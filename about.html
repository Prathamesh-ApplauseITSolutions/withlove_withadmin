For You

Established in 2016, For You Charitable Foundation embodies a passionate commitment to service — to uplift, heal, and connect. This not-for-profit organization actively engages in a myriad of seva activities, both small and large, across Pune and India.

Aligned with the belief, “Where there is a need, there will be — With Love,” the foundation serves as a bridge between hearts — uniting givers and receivers, compassion and action. It plays a pivotal role in fostering awareness among children about social responsibility and offers meaningful internship opportunities for college students to nurture future changemakers.

Recognized for its impactful contributions, the foundation has received prestigious accolades — including an award from Commissioner of Police Mr. Amitesh Kumar and Punekar News for making Pune a Vibrant City through Social Impact. Its projects — such as Sweetlings (funded by Reliance Foundation), Feed Forward,Joy Of Giving, Books Donation Drives, Ganesha Workshops, and the Kindness Marathon — have touched countless lives. Notably, the Stop Beggary project was chosen among the 100 Most Inspiring Stories in the Design For Change Challenge 2016.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

For You Charitable Foundation

At For You, we believe that the world needs more bridgers — people who bridge hearts, spread love, and create connection.We need those who awaken the best in others, who unify where there is division, whose love is contagious and liberatory where there is hate.

These are the miracle workers of our time. It is not only hard work, but heartwork to be a bridge. For You celebrates the many faces of love — fierce love, soft love, radical love, liberatory love, and the relentless love that refuses to give up on humanity. We see bridges as open channels for Divine Love to flow through — reminding us that compassion knows no boundaries.

For You Charitable Foundation strives to lend a helping hand that empowers communities and restores dignity to life. We aim to be the voice for those unheard, the strength for those unseen, and the light for those left behind. Our mission is to inspire generations to believe that “Being Kind Is Great Karma” — creating a lasting legacy of love, connection, and collective growth.
