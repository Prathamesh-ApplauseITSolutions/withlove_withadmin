Month of Execution: January 2024 onwards
Venue: Epiphany School
Beneficiaries: Underprivileged kids , Five Students from Epiphany School

Description

Students from Chatrabhuj Narsee School visited the labour camp, bringing tiffin boxes, bottles, and clothes for donation. Beyond material contributions, they shared kindness, presence, and heartfelt connection with the children, engaging in conversations, storytelling, and play. This initiative transformed a simple act of giving into a powerful lesson in gratitude and empathy, reinforcing the belief that small acts of kindness can spark meaningful change and inspire both givers and recipients alike.
