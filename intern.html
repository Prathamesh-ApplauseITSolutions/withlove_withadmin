Internship Program – With Love NGO

The internship program at With Love NGO offers a valuable chance to delve into the non-profit sector through hands-on grassroots work, enabling interns to make a meaningful contribution. In today's educational landscape, schools are actively promoting students' engagement with NGOs as part of their curriculum. Our commitment includes placing students in suitable areas, offering guidance, and providing hands-on support to help comprehend diverse aspects and contribute to various projects. Upon completion, interns receive a letter of accomplishment and recommendation from our NGO.

With Love provides top-tier internships that empower participants to:
  • Gain hands-on professional experience and acquire insights from experts.
  • Develop and refine existing as well as new skills within a structured framework.
  • Reinforce academic learning through practical application.
  • Contribute to diverse and impactful initiatives.
  • Cultivate a spirit of "seva," fostering sensitivity and responsibility toward moral duties.
Intake capacity:

As a policy, we induct 4–6 interns at designated intervals. However, it is subject to requirements and availability of resources. Interns need to commit to a minimum of four weeks to achieve continuity and tangible outcomes. The program begins with an induction providing knowledge of the NGO, followed by matching the intern's skills with current organizational needs.

Apply to be an Intern:

If you would like to intern with us, please fill the Internship Form and mail it to foryoucharitablefoundation@gmail.com along with your latest CV.
