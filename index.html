About Us

Born out of the passion for giving back to society, For You Charitable Foundation works towards empowering marginalized communities through meaningful and compassionate action. The organization is engaged in varied projects that positively impact lives, nurture dignity, and spark kindness. With an unwavering belief that “Kindness is Contagious,” the foundation aims to create inclusive growth and empathetic generations by addressing causes that reduce imbalance and spread awareness.

We are officially registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860 (Registration No. MAHA/1058/2016/Pune),and all contributions are eligible for 80G tax exemption, ensuring that every act of kindness is part of a structured, accountable, and transparent effort toward lasting change.
