Month of Execution: January 2024 onwards
Venue: Epiphany School
Beneficiaries: Underprivileged kids , Five Students from Epiphany School

Description

With Love NGO organized a declutter and donate campaign to encourage people to donate unused items from their homes. These collected items were then distributed to underprivileged children and families, providing them with essential resources while promoting sustainable living and community sharing. This initiative not only helped those in need but also created awareness about responsible consumption and the joy of giving.
